Lasagna

Description

Quick and easy homemade lasagna perfect for a quick weeknight meal

Ingredients

  • lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • can of tomatoes
  • ricotta cheese
  • mozarella cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the lasagna noodles for ten minutes
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees farenheit
  3. Cook beef until brown and drain.
  4. Simmer tomates and ground beef together for 20 min
  5. Spray a 9x13 casserole dish with cooking spray
  6. Arrange noodles in a single layer
  7. Top with a cup of meat and tomato mixture
  8. Add a layer of ricotta cheese
  9. Continue layering noodles, meat, cheese.
  10. Top with Mozarella cheese
  11. Bake in oven for 1 hr 30 min or until bubbly and cheese is brown
