Chicken Soup

Description

Chicken soup made with cream, spinach, carrots, and yummy gnocchi. This soup is very hearty and simmered on the stove until delicious.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked diced chicken
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1 cup chopped celery
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 3 cloves of garlic minced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup cream
  • 1 box of chicken broth
  • 1 pkg of potato gnocchi
  • 2 cups of spinach

Steps

  1. Sauted the celery, onion, and garlic in s dutch oven until translucent and golden.
  2. Add cream, broth, chicken, carrots, and gnocchi to the pot and cook until gnocchi is done.
  3. Add spinach and cover and remove from heat for 5 minutes. Server while warm.
